About Our Kerala Branch

Established in 2016, our Kerala branch is a serene healthcare facility nestled in God's Own Country. This 220-bed multi-specialty hospital combines traditional Ayurvedic wisdom with modern medical practices, providing holistic healthcare services to the residents of Kerala and visitors from around the world.
