Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prepare for my appointment?

Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. Bring your ID, insurance card, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results. If you're seeing a specialist, bring any referral forms if applicable.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment using the Reschedule/Cancel tab on this page. Please provide your booking ID and contact number. We request that you notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to reschedule or cancel.

How long will my appointment take?

Initial consultations typically last 30-45 minutes, while follow-up appointments usually take 15-30 minutes. However, the duration may vary depending on the nature of your visit and the specific department.

Do I need a referral to see a specialist?

Some specialists may require a referral from your primary care physician, while others accept direct appointments. This also depends on your insurance requirements. If you're unsure, please contact our appointment desk at +91 1234 567 890 for clarification.
