About Our Chennai Branch

Established in 2011, our Chennai branch is a premier healthcare facility in the cultural capital of South India. This 270-bed multi-specialty hospital combines traditional values with modern medical practices, providing comprehensive healthcare services to the residents of Chennai and surrounding areas.
