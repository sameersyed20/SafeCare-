Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the Family Card valid?

The SafeCare Family Card is valid for one year from the date of issue. You will receive a renewal notification 30 days before the expiry date.

Can I add or remove family members after getting the card?

Yes, you can add or remove family members during the validity period. Additional charges may apply when adding members, and no refunds are provided for removing members. Please contact our customer service for assistance.

How soon can I start using the benefits after applying?

Once your application is processed and payment is confirmed, you will receive a digital card via email within 24 hours. You can start using the benefits immediately with the digital card. The physical card will be delivered within 7 working days.

Are there any age restrictions for family members?

There are no age restrictions for family members. You can include members of any age, from newborns to elderly parents or grandparents.

Can I upgrade my card tier during the validity period?

Yes, you can upgrade your card tier during the validity period by paying the difference in the annual fee. Downgrades are only possible at the time of renewal.
