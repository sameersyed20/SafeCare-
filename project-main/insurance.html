Insurance Process

Understanding how to use your insurance at SafeCare Hospital.

1

Inform at Admission

Inform our insurance desk about your intention to avail cashless treatment at the time of admission.

2

Submit Documents

Submit your health insurance card, ID proof, and cashless request form provided by the hospital.

3

Pre-Authorization

Our insurance desk will coordinate with your insurance provider for pre-authorization.

4

Approval & Treatment

Once approved, you can proceed with the treatment without paying the covered amount.

5

Settlement

Pay only for expenses not covered by your insurance policy at the time of discharge.

Required Documents

  • Health Insurance Card/Policy Details
  • Photo ID Proof (Aadhar/PAN/Passport)
  • Cashless Request Form (provided by hospital)
  • TPA Card (if applicable)
1

Pay for Treatment

Pay for your medical expenses at the hospital and collect all original bills and documents.

2

Collect Documentation

Request for all necessary medical reports, discharge summary, and investigation reports.

3

File Claim

Submit the claim form along with all original documents to your insurance provider within the specified time frame.

4

Claim Processing

Your insurance provider will process the claim and may request additional information if needed.

5

Reimbursement

Once approved, the insurance provider will reimburse the eligible amount directly to your bank account.

Required Documents

  • Claim Form (provided by insurance company)
  • Original Hospital Bills and Receipts
  • Discharge Summary
  • Investigation Reports
  • Doctor's Prescription
  • Pharmacy Bills
  • Any other documents requested by the insurer
1

Planned Admission

For planned procedures, contact our insurance desk at least 48-72 hours before admission.

2

Submit Documents

Provide your insurance details, ID proof, and doctor's recommendation for the procedure.

3

Pre-Authorization Request

Our insurance desk will send a pre-authorization request to your insurance provider with estimated costs.

4

Approval

Wait for approval from the insurance provider before proceeding with the treatment.

5

Treatment & Settlement

Once approved, proceed with the treatment and settle any non-covered expenses at discharge.
