About Our Bengaluru Branch

Established in 2014, our Bengaluru branch is a tech-forward healthcare facility in India's Silicon Valley. This 280-bed multi-specialty hospital combines digital innovation with medical excellence, providing cutting-edge healthcare services to the tech-savvy residents of Bengaluru and surrounding areas.
