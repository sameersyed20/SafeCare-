About Our Hyderabad Branch

Established in 2005, our Hyderabad branch is the flagship hospital of SafeCare Hospital network. Located in the heart of Jubilee Hills, this 300-bed multi-specialty hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by some of the best medical professionals in the country.

Our Facilities

  • Advanced Cardiac Care Unit
  • Neuroscience Center
  • Maternity and Neonatal Care
  • 12 Modern Operation Theaters
  • Advanced Imaging Center (MRI, CT, PET-CT)
  • 24/7 Emergency Services
  • Fully Equipped Diagnostic Labs
  • Rehabilitation Center
