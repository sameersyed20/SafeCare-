About Our Mumbai Branch

Established in 2008, our Mumbai branch is a premier healthcare facility in the heart of India's financial capital. This 250-bed multi-specialty hospital offers comprehensive medical services with cutting-edge technology and a team of highly skilled healthcare professionals dedicated to providing exceptional patient care.
